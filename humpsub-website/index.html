Our Work

Over the past few years, the team has nearly doubled in size and its competitive performance has increased dramatically. At the 2015 International Submarine Races (ISR), we set a new UW record at 6.01 knots (placing 2nd in the male-piloted category) and our female pilot set a school record of 5.44 knots (placing 1st for women; a mere 0.06 knots from the ISR World Record).

Despite limited resources and workspace, our team of dedicated engineering students accomplished UW's best performance ever in competition. This year, the team is expanding, working in a new space provided by the UW Applied Physics Lab, and setting its sights on the world speed records.
